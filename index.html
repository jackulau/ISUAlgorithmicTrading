ABOUT

About

The Algorithmic Trading Club (ATC) at Iowa State University provides a collaborative environment where students can explore, develop, and apply algorithmic trading strategies. No prior trading experience is required.

01

Education

Weekly meetings with structured learning: 10 minutes of market discussion, 25 minutes on financial concepts, and 25 minutes of hands-on coding and software development.

02

Projects

Two major quant projects per semester with designated team leaders. Members develop trading algorithms and conduct simulated trading activities.

03

Competitions

Internal Investopedia trading challenges and travel to collegiate quant tournaments. We also host the Annual Iowa State Collegiate Quant Tournament.

04

Networking

Annual networking event in downtown Des Moines with Iowa Quants. Regular virtual discussions with quant professionals across the state. Field trips to quant finance firms.
